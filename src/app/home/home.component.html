How to Use the Eisenhower Matrix

The Eisenhower matrix, also known as the Urgent-Important matrix, helps you categorize tasks into four quadrants:

Urgent and Important

Do these tasks immediately.

Important, but Not Urgent

Schedule these tasks for later.

Urgent, but Not Important

Delegate these tasks to someone else if possible.

Not Urgent and Not Important

Eliminate or postpone these tasks.

By using this matrix, you can prioritize tasks effectively and focus on what truly matters.
